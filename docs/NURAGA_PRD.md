## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### Overview
**Nuraga - Integrated Safety Intelligence** adalah platform SaaS berbasis web yang mengdigitalisasi dan mengoptimalkan manajemen Keselamatan dan Kesehatan Kerja (K3/HSE) di organisasi industri. Platform ini mengintegrasikan incident tracking, hazard management, work permits, AI-powered fatigue monitoring, dan gamification untuk menciptakan budaya keselamatan kerja yang proaktif dan mencapai target **Zero Accident**.

### Value Proposition
- **Untuk Pekerja:** Aplikasi mudah digunakan, sistem reward yang memotivasi, monitoring kesehatan real-time
- **Untuk Supervisor:** Oversight tim, approval workflow, monitoring compliance
- **Untuk HSE Officer:** Analytics mendalam, trend forecasting, audit automation, compliance management
- **Untuk Manager:** Dashboard strategis, ROI tracking, performance metrics, strategic decision support

### Key Business Metrics
- **Target Market Size:** 50,000+ companies di Indonesia dengan >100 employees
- **TAM (Total Addressable Market):** Rp 2.5 Trillion annually (K3 compliance + incident reduction)
- **Expected adoption rate Year 1:** 500 pilot customers
- **Revenue target Year 1:** Rp 15 Billion (subscription-based)

## 1. KONTEKS BISNIS

### 1.1 Problem Statement

#### Current State
Indonesia menghadapi **tantangan serius** dalam manajemen K3:
- **Tingkat accident rate tinggi:** Rata-rata 100,000+ kecelakaan kerja/tahun di Indonesia
- **Underreporting:** Hanya ~30% incidents yang tercatat formal (sisanya "diselesaikan lokal")
- **Manual processes:** Data tracking masih di spreadsheet, WhatsApp, atau kertas (prone to error)
- **Compliance gaps:** Banyak perusahaan tidak fully compliant dengan UU No. 13 Tahun 2003
- **Limited visibility:** Management tidak punya real-time visibility terhadap safety status
- **Reactive culture:** Organisasi hanya respond setelah incident terjadi (bukan preventive)
- **High cost:** Setiap incident = lost productivity, medical cost, legal liability, insurance premium increase

#### Pain Points per Role

**Staff/Worker:**
- Sulit melaporkan hazard (tidak ada platform formal)
- Tidak tahu fatigue level → work unsafe condition
- Unclear safety status & requirements
- Motivasi rendah karena kontribusi K3 tidak appreciate

**Supervisor:**
- Monitoring attendance & safety manual
- Approval workflow tidak structured → delays
- Tidak punya data untuk make informed decision
- Communication hazard/incident kurang efektif

**HSE Officer:**
- Audit manual → time consuming, inconsistent
- Sulit track corrective action progress
- Data silos → cannot analyze trends
- Compliance reporting manual & error-prone
- Difficulty predicting & preventing incidents

**Manager:**
- Tidak punya KPI dashboard untuk safety metrics
- Risk assessment untuk strategic decision sulit
- Insurance claim processing complicated
- TRIR/LTI calculation manual & inaccurate

## 4.4 Strategi AI & Data Science

### 4.4.1 Visi AI & Predictive Analytics

**Nuraga mengintegrasikan AI & Machine Learning untuk transformasi dari "reactive" menjadi "predictive" safety management.**

Melalui teknologi kecerdasan buatan, Nuraga tidak hanya mencatat insiden tetapi **memprediksi risiko sebelum terjadi**, memberikan rekomendasi proaktif, dan mengidentifikasi pattern yang manusia tidak terlihat.

### 4.4.2 Komponen AI Utama (Roadmap)

#### Phase 1 (v1.0-1.1): Fatigue Risk Prediction [CORE]
**Tujuan:** Prediksi tingkat kelelahan pekerja dengan akurasi >85% untuk early intervention.

**Fitur:**
- Machine Learning model berbasis XGBoost/Neural Network
- Input: Sleep hours, stress level, cumulative work hours, shift pattern, environmental factors
- Output: Fatigue Risk Score (0-100) → Classification (GREEN/YELLOW/RED)
- Real-time inference saat check-in (<100ms latency)
- Rekomendasi personalized (rest, buddy system, mandatory break)

**Bisnis Impact:**
- Reduce incident saat worker fatigue tinggi sebesar 30-40%
- Early warning system untuk supervisor
- Proactive intervention sebelum accident

#### Phase 2 (v2.0): Incident Prediction & Risk Scoring [STRATEGIC]
**Tujuan:** Memprediksi probabilitas incident dalam 7 hari ke depan per area/departemen.

**Fitur:**
- Predictive model menggunakan hazard trends, incident history, environmental factors
- Output: Risk Score per location, predicted incident count, incident type probability
- Anomaly detection untuk pattern yang tidak normal
- Correlation analysis: hazard report → actual incident conversion rate
- Location hotspot identification & trending

**Bisnis Impact:**
- 7-hari advance warning untuk high-risk situations
- Resource allocation optimization (focus pada high-risk areas)
- Prevention-focused safety management
- Insurance company interest untuk risk profiling

#### Phase 3 (v2.0+): AI-Assisted Root Cause Analysis [INTELLIGENCE]
**Tujuan:** Assist HSE Officer dengan AI suggestions untuk 5-Whys analysis.

**Fitur:**
- NLP-based (BERT) untuk understanding incident descriptions
- Knowledge base dari 500+ documented incident-RCA pairs
- AI suggest likely root cause categories berdasarkan incident pattern
- Interactive wizard dengan AI recommendations per Why level
- Learning dari user corrections (continuous improvement)

**Bisnis Impact:**
- Standardisasi RCA process, reduce bias
- Faster investigation completion
- Better root cause identification
- Prevent recurring incidents

#### Phase 4 (v2.1+): Advanced Analytics & Benchmarking [SCALE]
**Tujuan:** Provide benchmarking & comparative analysis untuk strategic decisions.

**Fitur:**
- Anonymized industry benchmarking (compare TRIR vs peers)
- Safety culture maturity scoring & forecasting
- Predictive modeling untuk compliance readiness
- Cost-benefit analysis untuk safety investments
- ROI calculator untuk incident prevention initiatives

**Bisnis Impact:**
- Attract large enterprises untuk benchmark usage
- New revenue stream dari analytics services
- Strategic partnerships dengan government agencies

### 5.2 Key Use Cases (Detailed Flows)

#### UC-1: Complete Work Permit Lifecycle

**Actors:** Staff, Supervisor, HSE Officer, Manager

**Precondition:** Planned maintenance work identified

**Flow:**
1. Staff submit work permit request
   - Select permit type (Confined Space, Hot Work, etc)
   - Describe work, location, duration
   - List workers, equipment, potential hazards
   - Identify required APD
2. Supervisor review & approve
   - Check completeness
   - Add safety requirements
   - Forward to HSE Officer
3. HSE Officer technical review
   - Validate hazard assessment
   - Order gas test jika required
   - Approve or request clarification
4. Manager final approval
   - Business sign-off
   - Approve (permit becomes ACTIVE)
5. Permit execution
   - Worker acknowledge pre-work briefing
   - Daily start: check permit validity, gas test results
   - Daily end: verify no incidents, work completed
6. Permit closure
   - Work completed, sign-off
   - Archive permit with completion report

**Postcondition:** Permit data available for audit & analytics
